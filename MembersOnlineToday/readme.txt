[b][size=12pt]Description[/size][/b]

Members Online Today displays a list of members who have logged in within a configurable time period. The list appears as a block in the Info Center on the Board Index page, below the standard "Users Online" block.

[b][size=12pt]Features[/size][/b]

[list]
[li]Shows members who have been online today, in the last 24 hours, or in the last 7 days[/li]
[li]Configurable sort order (by name or last login time, ascending or descending)[/li]
[li]Configurable visibility (everyone, registered members, or staff only)[/li]
[li]Respects member "show online" privacy setting — hidden members are only visible to moderators[/li]
[li]Displays membergroup colors[/li]
[li]Hover tooltip shows last login time for each member[/li]
[li]Visible/hidden member count breakdown for moderators[/li]
[/list]

[b][size=12pt]Settings[/size][/b]

After installation, configure the mod at:
[b]Admin > Configuration > Modification Settings > General[/b]

Available settings:
[list]
[li][b]Sort members by[/b] — Last login time or Member name[/li]
[li][b]Sort direction[/b] — Ascending or Descending[/li]
[li][b]Time range[/b] — Today (since midnight), Last 24 hours, or Last 7 days[/li]
[li][b]Who can see the member list[/b] — Everyone, Registered members only, or Staff only[/li]
[/list]

[b][size=12pt]Compatibility[/size][/b]

[li]PHP 7.4+ (PHP 8.1+ recommended)[/li]
[/list]
